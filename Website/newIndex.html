Welcome to the BWIBot Coding Space!
Here you can write simple programs for the BWIBot in three easy steps:
1. Type your program by clicking the various command buttons.
2. Hit "Generate QR Code" to turn your program into a QR Code.
3. Hold your QR code up to BWIBot camera and watch your program run!
